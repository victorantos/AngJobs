## Hi there

I'm Victor, a software developer based in Europe. I built AngJobs to make it easier to browse and apply to jobs from Hacker News's monthly "Who is hiring?" threads.

## Why AngJobs?

The HN job threads are great but hard to navigate — hundreds of comments, no search, no way to track what you've seen. AngJobs solves that by:

- **Organizing jobs by month** — easy to browse and compare
- **Clean formatting** — readable job descriptions
- **Direct applications** — apply straight to hiring managers
