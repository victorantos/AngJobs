Roles:

- Senior Software Engineer, 3D Measurements

- Engineering Manager, 3D Measurements

The TL;DR: We are writing software that generates 3D models of buildings. Homeowners, contractors, and insurance professionals use those models to derive metrics, estimate cost and visualize changes (think kitchen remodel). Our long term ambition is to have an accurate and up to date 3D model (digital twin) of every building on the planet.

Hover is expanding the team on many fronts. The 3D Measurements team in particular is looking for talented &amp; experienced software engineers that have proven experience designing and implementing systems for 3D modeling, reconstruction, or CAD-related software and that bring a solid foundation in geometry and 3D math.
