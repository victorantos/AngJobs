Grafana Labs - Senior&#x2F;Staff Software Engineer - AI &#x2F; Agentic apps - 100% Remote (US + Canada) -

Im the hiring manager - some of the product highlights under the team&#x27;s scope:
- Grafana Assistant: the actually useful AI assistant that helps you do everything in Grafana; it works everywhere - even in OSS!
- Investigations: background agents for investigating issues
- Grafana Cloud CLI (GCX): CLI covering the entire surface of Grafana Cloud&#x2F;OSS APIs; plays great with Claude Code&#x2F;Codex&#x2F;Cursor and the Assistant
- AI Observability: helps you build useful agents and then lets you monitor, eval and improve how they behave
- O11y Bench: compare agents&#x27; performance across a range of observability&#x2F;SRE tasks

Our pace of development and innovation is RAPID - the AI Observability product went from being the winning hackathon project to public preview in 6 weeks. We are at the cutting edge here - we are experimenting with autonomous (but safe) SREs, an agentic platform, improving agentic dev experience with o11y insights, fine-tuned LLMs for specific tasks, and more.

We are looking for highly ambitious engineers who have a great sense of ownership, are all-in on agentic development, and have previously built and operated systems at scale. We are all full-stack and love initiative- we would love to talk with you _especially_ if you built something out in the open!
