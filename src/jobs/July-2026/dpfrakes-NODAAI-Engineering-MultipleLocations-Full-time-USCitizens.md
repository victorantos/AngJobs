NODA AI is the defense software company that built and fielded the original vendor-agnostic autonomy orchestrator. Founded in 2024 and headquartered in Austin, Texas, NODA builds the software layer that enables heterogeneous uncrewed systems to coordinate, adapt, and execute complex missions using human intent across contested multi-domain environments. NODA’s orchestration software is operationally deployed, hardware- and software-agnostic, and purpose-built for the complexity of modern autonomous warfare.
