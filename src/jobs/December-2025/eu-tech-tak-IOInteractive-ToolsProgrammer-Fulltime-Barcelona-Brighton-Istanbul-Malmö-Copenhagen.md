IO Interactive - Tools Programmer - Full time - Barcelona, Brighton, Istanbul, Malmö, Copenhagen

IO Interactive is looking for a skilled Tools Programmer to join our Backbone team and help power the tech behind our games. You’ll be working across multiple flagship projects - our James Bond game, 007 First Light, our ambitious Project Fantasy, and the HITMAN universe - building and evolving tools in our proprietary Glacier engine. Together with artists, designers, and programmers, you’ll create robust, user-friendly workflows that let our teams move faster, experiment more, and bring world-class gameplay to life.

What you will do:

<pre><code>    - Collaborate to design and upgrade the Glacier editor and tools, empowering content creators.
    - Engage with users to understand production requirements and streamline workflows.
    - Customize and integrate tools to meet project needs.
    - Create user-friendly interfaces for efficient usage.
    - Integrate tools with version control systems.
    - Stay updated on the latest technologies.
    - Ensure high quality through testing and security measures.
    - Continuously improve tools based on feedback and project demands.
</code></pre>
Who you are:

<pre><code>    - Have experience working in the gaming industry.
    - Proficient in C# and C++ with strong code design skills. Familiarity with WPF or similar UI technologies preferred.
    - Capable of task breakdown, estimation, and participating in task assessments.
    - Strong collaboration and communication skills, adept at discerning user needs.
    - Bachelor&#x27;s degree in Computer Science or equivalent required. Master&#x27;s degree preferred.
</code></pre>
Who we are:

IO Interactive is an independent videogame development and publishing company with studios in Copenhagen, Malmö, Barcelona, Istanbul and Brighton. As the creative force behind some of the most talked-about multiplatform video games in the last decade, we are committed to creating unforgettable characters and experiences all powered by our award-winning, proprietary Glacier technology.
