Laminar Engineering - Full-Stack CV&#x2F;ML Systems Engineer - Contract - REMOTE

Laminar Engineering builds real-time computer vision systems for airborne detection and tracking of drones, combining multispectral sensing and edge inference. We’re delivering this for a client, and we’re looking for a versatile engineer to join the R&amp;D effort. This is a role for someone who thrives with autonomy when given a clear end state. More details to be shared with the right candidates.

The role spans the whole pipeline, from data to deployed system: CV&#x2F;ML on NVIDIA edge devices (Jetson &#x2F; TensorRT &#x2F; DeepStream), real-time video processing and multi-object tracking, camera&#x2F;gimbal control, dataset collection and curation, model training and evaluation, backend infrastructure and APIs, and operator-facing dashboards. You’d own deployed vision systems end to end, including diagnosing and fixing real-world failures in live video.

Strong fit: applied CV and perception, including tracking, real-time pipelines, and NVIDIA edge deployment, plus strong Python and comfort across the stack. Relevant experience: PyTorch, TensorRT&#x2F;ONNX, DeepStream&#x2F;GStreamer, embedded Linux, and frontend work for operator tooling.

Remote contract, flexible hours, some overlap with UTC-05 preferred.

Email hiring@laminr.co and mention HN.
