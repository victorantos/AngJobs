Chime is a financial technology company focused on helping everyday Americans unlock financial progress. We built Chime on the premise that core banking services should be helpful, easy, and free, and our security teams play a direct role in protecting the products and infrastructure that members rely on.

Our Product Security org works across application and infrastructure security, security design and architecture reviews, and vulnerability management. We partner closely with engineering teams to identify, prioritize, and remediate risk early, with an emphasis on making security practical, embedded in everyday development, and useful to the teams building Chime’s products. If you’re excited about being on the front lines of securing both AI and financial technology, this is a particularly interesting place to do that work. We currently have Senior Application Security Engineer, Product Security Engineer, and Manager - Product Security open.

If none of those are quite the right fit, feel free to apply anyway! We expect to open more security roles in the near future across a range of experience levels and specializations.
