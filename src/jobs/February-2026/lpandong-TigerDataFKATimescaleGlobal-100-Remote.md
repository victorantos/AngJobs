Tiger Data (FKA Timescale) [ Global - 100% Remote - Multiple Roles: Eng, GTM, Support + more ]
We began in 2017 as TimescaleDB, focused on making PostgreSQL the best database for time-series data. As our customers evolved, so did we.

Today, more than 2,000 companies use our technology to power real-time analytics, agentic applications, and high-ingest operational systems. What started as a time-series database has become the fastest PostgreSQL for modern workloads. To reflect that evolution, we’re now TigerData—a name that speaks to our speed, precision, and ambition. The tiger has long been part of our culture: fast, focused, powerful. From “Tiger Time” all-hands to welcoming new hires as “tiger cubs,” it’s how we build—decisively, fearlessly, and with purpose.

This is not a reinvention: it’s a reflection of how we already serve our customers today.
