CLUTCH - Embedded Systems Engineer (Computer Vision), Product Designer, Head of Growth - Remote - Full-time

Clutch builds an AI camera for racket sports courts and an app that generates automated highlight reels and match analysis using computer vision.

Our initial focus is padel (the fastest-growing sport globally). Since launching in 2025, we’ve deployed the Clutch Cam to clubs in 25+ countries.

We’re a small, early-stage team building both hardware and software.
