Medtronic - Principal &#x2F; Senior Software Engineers - Metro Boston Area, MA - Onsite

We’re hiring principal-level software engineers to join the former Affera team — now part of Medtronic — building next-generation cardiac electrophysiology (EP) mapping and ablation systems.

The Affera Mapping and Ablation System is designed to treat patients with cardiac arrhythmias through advanced mapping and integrated therapy delivery. The platform creates detailed, real-time visualizations of the heart’s electrical activity, enabling physicians to identify abnormal areas. Once identified, the system delivers targeted ablation energy to disrupt those signals and restore normal rhythm.

As part of Medtronic’s EP portfolio, we’re scaling and advancing this technology into the next generation of integrated mapping and generator systems.

We’re looking for Senior and Principal engineers with either:

- Desktop UI focus (Qt&#x2F;QML or similar frameworks), or

- Core systems focus (C++&#x2F;Python, real-time signals processing, ROS, Linux, hardware integration).
