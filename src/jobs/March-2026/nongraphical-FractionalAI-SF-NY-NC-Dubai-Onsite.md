Fractional AI - Senior&#x2F;Staff Software Engineer - SF, NY, NC, Dubai - Onsite

We’re a team of experienced generalist SWEs and AI enthusiasts who are building custom AI agents for enterprise clients. You’ll work on 3-4 different projects a year and play a central role on each one.  No AI experience required: we are actively trying to be the best place in the world to learn applied AI.

* We’re “selling pickaxes during the gold rush”. When AI finds its next killer app, we’ll hopefully be the first to know about it.
