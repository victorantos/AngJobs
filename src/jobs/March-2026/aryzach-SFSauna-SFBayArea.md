SF Sauna -- SF Bay Area

Electrical &#x2F; Power Systems Engineer - Build a battery-integrated sauna heater

Paid contract role, $80 - 150&#x2F;hr, 15–25 hr initial scope

If there’s mutual interest and the prototype goes well, this could extend into deeper collaboration or a more dedicated role.

A short description of the EE project you&#x27;re most proud of building from scratch
A link (GitHub, project photos, writeup, resume, etc)
