Decisionly - Senior Software Engineer (Backend) - Full Time - Remote (US based)

Decisionly is building AI-powered dispute decisioning for card issuers, eliminating cost and enabling compliant, scalable card operations. Decisionly automates complex manual workflows end-to-end, modernizing payment infrastructure and generating unique first-party fraud insights for fintechs, processors and banks.

Our founding team has a strong track record and unique founder-market fit. We founded Chargehound (backed by Y Combinator, Khosla), the first automated dispute solution for merchants like Airbnb, Uber and Doordash. PayPal acquired Chargehound in 2021. We&#x27;ve worked together since 2016.

We&#x27;re looking for someone who is excited about the idea of working with a small team to solve a real pain point for credit card issuers. You&#x27;ll need to have the ability to think from both a technical and product perspective to help translate a complex business process into a scalable, automated system.
