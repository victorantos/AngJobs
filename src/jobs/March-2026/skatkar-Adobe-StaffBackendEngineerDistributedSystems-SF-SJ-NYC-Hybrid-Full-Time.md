Adobe - Staff Backend Engineer (Distributed Systems) - SF, SJ, NYC - Hybrid - Full-Time

Adobe is building a Real-Time Customer Data Platform (CDP) that operates at a scale most companies never see. We aren&#x27;t just building another &quot;enterprise app&quot;; we are managing trillions of events and solving complex state problems in real-time.

If you find &quot;distributed systems&quot; a lifestyle rather than a buzzword, we want to talk. We are currently tackling challenges in high-throughput streaming, identity resolution at scale, and sub-millisecond data retrieval.

Core: Java, Scala, Spring Boot

Compute&#x2F;Stream: Spark, Flink, K8s

Storage&#x2F;Messaging: Kafka, FoundationDB, Aerospike, Cosmos DB, Delta Lake

Infrastructure: Multi-cloud (Azure&#x2F;AWS), Docker, Vector DBs

Concurrency &amp; Performance: You don’t just write code; you tune it. You understand the JVM, memory models, and how to squeeze performance out of every thread.

Architecture: Experience with Flink&#x2F;Kafka&#x2F;Spark isn&#x27;t just about using the API—it&#x27;s about knowing how they fail and how to keep them running under massive load.
