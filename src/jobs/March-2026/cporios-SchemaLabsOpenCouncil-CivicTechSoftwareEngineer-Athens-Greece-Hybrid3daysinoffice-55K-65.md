Schema Labs (OpenCouncil) - Civic Tech Software Engineer - Athens, Greece - Hybrid (3 days in office) - €55K-65K

We&#x27;re a 4-person non-profit building OpenCouncil (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;opencouncil.gr" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;opencouncil.gr</a>), an AI platform that makes Greek municipal council meetings accessible to citizens. We take hours-long council sessions and turn them into searchable, per-topic video clips, SMS&#x2F;email notifications, and speaker analytics.

We&#x27;re live with 10 municipalities including Athens, and the project is open source: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;schemalabz&#x2F;opencouncil" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;github.com&#x2F;schemalabz&#x2F;opencouncil</a>

Stack: TypeScript, Next.js, React, PostgreSQL, Elasticsearch. Heavy use of LLMs for transcription, speaker ID, and turning unstructured PDFs&#x2F;transcripts into structured data. We use Claude Code, Cursor, and similar tools daily.

Looking for: 2+ years experience, strong web dev skills, genuine interest in civic tech. Bonus if you&#x27;re strong in either data engineering&#x2F;LLM integration or frontend&#x2F;UX. Must be based in (or willing to relocate to) Athens, fluent in Greek and English.

We offer up to 30% annual profit sharing, lunch on office days, and up to 3 days&#x2F;week remote. Small team, high ownership, real impact.
