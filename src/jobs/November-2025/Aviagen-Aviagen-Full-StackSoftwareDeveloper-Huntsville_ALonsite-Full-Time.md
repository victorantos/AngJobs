Aviagen - Full-Stack Software Developer - Huntsville, AL (onsite) - Full-Time

Aviagen, a global poultry-breeding company supplying pedigree lines for broiler production, is looking for a Full-Stack Software Developer who loves building tools that users actually use and benefit from.

What you’ll do:

- Architect and build web apps that replace legacy systems, streamline business processes, or replace paper or excel based workflows. So far, our web-apps are vanilla JS, with C# &#x2F; ASP.NET Core backends. We&#x27;re open to JS frameworks where appropriate.

- Collaborate directly with stakeholders to identify pain points and deliver impactful solutions.

- Own the end-to-end lifecycle: idea, design, implementation, deployment, support.

What we need:

- 5+ years of software development experience, comfortable across front-end and back-end.

- Solid JavaScript&#x2F;TypeScript skills, experience with REST APIs, relational databases.

- Enthusiastic about owning features, interacting with users, improving business workflows.

What we offer:

- Competitive salary + bonus + company benefits (details on request).

- Real ownership and visibility within a global company with the feel of a smaller team.

- Located onsite in Huntsville, AL - full-time. (Hybrid&#x2F;remote not currently available.)
