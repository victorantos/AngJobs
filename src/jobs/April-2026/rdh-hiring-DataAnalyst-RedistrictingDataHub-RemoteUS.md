Data Analyst - Redistricting Data Hub - Remote (US)

The Redistricting Data Hub provides individuals, good government organizations, and community groups the data, resources, and knowledge to participate effectively in the redistricting process. In service of this mission, we host thousands of datasets in all 50 states, and continue to add new data that is useful for map drawing and analysis.

We are currently hiring a full-time data analyst to join our team. In this role, you will work with geospatial data, election results, and other key datasets. We&#x27;re seeking candidates who are comfortable working remotely on a small team, skilled in data processing and analysis, and experienced with Python.

Applications will be accepted through Friday, May 1, 2026.
