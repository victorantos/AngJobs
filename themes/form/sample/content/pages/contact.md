## Your first class is free

Pick anything marked *All levels* or *Beginner* on the [schedule](/schedule/),
come ten minutes early, and say you're new at the desk. That's the entire
process. Wear anything you can move in; mats, blocks, and kettlebells live
here. The only thing worth bringing is a water bottle.
