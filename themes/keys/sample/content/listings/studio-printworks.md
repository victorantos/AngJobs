The top-floor corner of the old Printworks conversion: one room that does
everything, and does it with a five-meter ceiling and cast-iron windows on
two walls.

**The facts:** 44 m² studio with a proper sleeping mezzanine (standing
height at the bed, stairs not a ladder), fitted kitchen along one wall,
bathroom with a window — rare in this building — and a storage cage in
the basement. Leasehold, 96 years remaining; service charge €140/month
with a lift and a well-run residents' association.

**The area:** Foundry Lane — the Printworks café downstairs opens at 7,
the market square is six minutes, and the number 11 stops outside.

**The catch, honestly:** it's one room, and the mezzanine suits people who
don't share a bedtime with a light sleeper. Heating is electric — bills
run higher than gas; the association is quoting a communal heat-pump
scheme for 2027.
