The ground floor of a converted brewery cooperage, with its own front door
off the lane — no shared hallway — and a walled garden that belongs to
this flat alone.

**The facts:** two bedrooms (one double, one honest single that takes a
desk and a guest bed), a bright kitchen-living room with the original
beam, and 40 m² of walled garden facing west — evening sun, morning
shade. Share of freehold; service charge €90/month covering the roof and
buildings insurance; no lift, no stairs either.

**The area:** riverside — the towpath five minutes' walk for runners and
cyclists, two good cafés closer than that, and the footbridge puts the
station at eleven minutes.

**The catch, honestly:** the second bedroom is a single, whatever wide-angle
photos elsewhere suggest — we've measured it and it's in the floor plan.
And garden walls mean garden shade in winter.
