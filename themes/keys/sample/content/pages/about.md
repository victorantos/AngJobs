Harber & Voss is Mina Harber and Pieter Voss, an office on Chandler Street
with a dog in it, and no plans to become a chain.

Mina spent nine years at a big-brand agency and left over a listing she
wasn't allowed to write honestly. Pieter surveyed buildings for a living
and still reads a house the way surveyors do — which is why our
particulars mention the roof's age and the boiler's service history
instead of "deceptively spacious."

## The record

Last year: 34 sales, a median of 19 days from listing to offer, and — the
number we're proudest of — two sales we talked *out* of: owners we advised
to wait, repaint, or re-mortgage instead. Both came back. That's the
business model: the long game, played straight.

## The dog

Bruno. He's a lurcher, he attends all valuations, and he has never once
leaked a price.
