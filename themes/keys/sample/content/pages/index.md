We're a two-person agency with one rule that does all our marketing:
**every listing tells you the catch.** The downstairs bathroom, the
Saturday-morning hum, the trains you'll hear from the garden — it's in
our particulars, in writing, before you've booked a viewing. You'd find
it anyway; finding it from us is faster and builds the kind of trust that
sells the next house.

**Buying:** [what's on the market](/listings/) is current to the hour —
when something goes under offer, the flag changes the same day. Tell us
what you're actually after (the real list, not the portal filters) and
you'll hear about houses before the board goes up.

**Selling:** we photograph honestly, price straight, and answer our own
phone — **+1 555 0170**, human on the other end, Mon–Sat. Valuations are
free and come with the same honesty the buyers get; sometimes the honest
advice is "not yet," and we'll say so.
