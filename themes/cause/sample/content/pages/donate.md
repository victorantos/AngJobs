Tutors give the hours. Your money buys everything around the table.

| What a gift buys | |
| --- | --- |
| £10 | A learner's first three books — theirs to keep |
| £25 | Childcare for one session, the #1 reason learners stay |
| £60 | A neighborhood room for a month of evenings |
| £150 | Trains one new tutor, good for ~100 learner-hours a year |

## By bank transfer

Page & Chapter, sort code 00-00-00, account 00000000, reference: your name.
For Gift Aid (adds 25% at no cost to you), email
[hello@example.org](mailto:hello@example.org) after giving and we'll send
the one-line form.

## Other ways that count the same

A monthly £5 beats most one-off gifts — it's what lets us promise a learner
eighteen months. Employers often match donations; ask yours. And if money
is tight: [volunteer](/programs/) — four evenings of training and you're
someone's Tuesday.
