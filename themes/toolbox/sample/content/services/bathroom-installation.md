A bathroom is the one room where plumbing mistakes hide behind tiles for years before they announce themselves through the ceiling below. It's worth doing once, properly.

## What we take on

Straight replacements, full refits, walk-in showers, and the awkward jobs: moving the soil stack, boxing pipework properly, or making a downstairs toilet out of a cupboard that never quite worked.

## How the job runs

You get an itemised written quote — labour, materials, disposal — before anything is ordered. We agree the schedule up front and tell you which days the water will be off (usually hours, not days). At handover we walk the room together: every joint tested, every seal checked, every question answered before we invoice.
