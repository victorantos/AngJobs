The cheapest boiler repair is the one you never need. An annual service catches the tired parts in September, when it's a planned visit — not in January, when it's an emergency and the whole street's boilers went down in the same cold snap.

## Servicing

A proper service is a strip-down and inspection, not a vacuum and a new sticker. We check the burner, heat exchanger, seals, and safety devices, take flue readings, and leave you the numbers in writing. Takes about an hour.

## Repairs

Whatever make you have, we've probably had it apart. If a part is needed, you get the price — parts and labour, one number — before we order it. If the honest answer is that the boiler owes you nothing and a repair is throwing money at it, we'll say that too.
