A burst pipe doesn't wait for business hours, so neither do we. Call the main number any time — after hours it rings through to whoever is on duty.

## What happens when you call

First we get the water under control: we'll talk you to your stop valve on the phone, which usually turns a flood into a puddle before we've left the yard. Then we come and make it safe — stopped, capped, or contained.

## What it costs

The call-out covers getting to you and making the problem safe. If the permanent fix is quick, we usually do it there and then inside the same price. If it's bigger — a corroded run that needs replacing, a cylinder on its way out — you get a firm written quote before we go any further. No work starts on a guess.
