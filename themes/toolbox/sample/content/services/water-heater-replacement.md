Water heaters rarely fail politely. If yours is past ten years old, runs lukewarm, or has started making kettle noises, it's cheaper to replace it on your schedule than on its own.

## How we size it

Bigger isn't better — an oversized heater costs you money every day it runs. We ask how many people are in the house, how many bathrooms, and when everyone showers, and size it to that. You get two or three options with firm all-in prices: unit, fitting, and disposal in one number.

## The swap

Most straight swaps are done in a day. We drain and disconnect the old unit, fit and commission the new one, check every joint under pressure, and take the old heater away. You're back in hot water by dinner.
