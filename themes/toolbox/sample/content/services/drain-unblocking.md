A blocked drain is one of the few jobs where the price is nearly always the price: most blockages clear on the first visit with rods or the machine, inside the standard rate.

## What we clear

Kitchen sinks, bathroom basins, baths and showers, toilets, and outside gullies. Grease, scale, wipes (they are never flushable, whatever the packet says), and the occasional toy dinosaur.

## When it keeps coming back

A drain that blocks every few months isn't unlucky — it's telling you something. Usually a bellied pipe, a bad fall, or root intrusion. If we think that's what you've got, we'll say so and quote the proper fix separately. We won't just keep taking the call-out fee.
