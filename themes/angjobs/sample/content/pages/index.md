This paper covers one town and covers it properly: the harbor board and the
school gates, the market and the lifeboat station, plus the occasional
anonymous mosaicist. No wire copy, no syndicated filler — if it's in these
pages, someone from here stood there and wrote it down.

Start with the **[front page](/news/)**. Stories land as they're filed, not
on a schedule, and the [RSS feed](/news/rss.xml) carries everything.

Tips, corrections, and letters: see [contact](/contact/) — the phone gets
answered by a journalist, because there isn't anyone else here.
