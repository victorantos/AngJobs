Dental pain doesn't book ahead. Every weekday morning we hold slots for
people who woke up needing one, and the emergency number on every page of
this site reaches a real person outside opening hours.

## What counts as urgent

Toothache that kept you awake, a swelling in your face, a broken tooth with a
sharp edge, a knocked-out tooth, or a lost crown on a front tooth before a
big week. If you're not sure, call anyway — the person answering will know.

## What happens

The urgent visit does two jobs: gets you out of pain today, and gives you a
plain-words plan (with written costs) for the proper fix. Nobody is upsold
anything while they're hurting — that's a rule, not a slogan.

## What it costs

The urgent visit is one flat fee, told to you on the phone before you come
in. Out-of-hours callouts cost more and we say the number on the call, so
the decision is yours before you get in the car.
