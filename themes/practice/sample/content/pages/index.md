## Welcome

We're a small practice at 14 Alder Street: two dentists, one hygienist, and a front desk that answers the phone. We keep appointments unhurried, we explain everything before we do it, and we never start work without telling you what it costs.

If it's been years since you saw a dentist, that's fine — it's most of the new patients we meet. Nobody here will lecture you. We'll just look, tell you honestly where things stand, and agree on a plan one step at a time.

## What a first visit looks like

You'll sit down with a dentist, not a form. We look, we listen, and we talk through what we find in plain words — no jargon, no pressure. Most first visits take about forty minutes, and you leave knowing exactly what (if anything) needs doing and what it would cost.

[See how we can help](/care/) or [book an appointment](/contact/).

## Nervous about the dentist?

Say so when you book — it changes how we plan your visit. We go at your pace: we explain each step before it happens, we agree a stop signal, and nothing happens without your say-so. Many of our regulars once dreaded coming. They'll tell you it passes.
