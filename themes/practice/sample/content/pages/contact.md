## Book or ask

Call **[+1 555 0104](tel:+1 555 0104)** — Rosa answers Monday to Friday 8:00–18:00 and Saturday 9:00–13:00. No phone menus, no hold music. If you'd rather not call, drop in during opening hours and we'll find you a time at the desk.

**In pain outside those hours?** Call **[+1 555 0199](tel:+1 555 0199)** and you'll reach whichever of our dentists is on duty. Don't sit up all night with a toothache — that's what the line is for.

## Where we are

**14 Alder Street, Fairview** — the blue-green door between the pharmacy and the bakery, two minutes' walk from the Market Square bus stop (routes 4, 11 and 16).

## Parking

Free patient parking behind the building — turn in at the pharmacy side and follow the wall. Two spaces by the back door are reserved for patients who can't walk far; ask Rosa to hold one when you book. Street parking on Alder Street is free for the first hour.

## Access

- Step-free entrance at the back door, and the treatment rooms are all on the ground floor
- The back door and corridor take wheelchairs and mobility scooters comfortably
- A hearing loop at the front desk — tell us if you'd like us to use it in the treatment room too
- Happy to book a double-length appointment if transfers or moving takes you a little longer — just say when booking
