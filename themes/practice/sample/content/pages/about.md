Alder Street Dental has been at number 14 since 2009. We're deliberately small: we'd rather know our patients than grow the waiting room. Appointments run on time because we book fewer of them, and the dentist who starts your treatment is the one who finishes it.

## How we work

- **Plain words.** We say "a small hole starting in a back tooth", not the Latin for it.
- **Prices before work.** You get the cost in writing before anything happens, every time.
- **Your pace.** Nervous patients set the speed. Stopping is always allowed.

## Who you'll meet

**Dr. Mara Ellison** owns the practice and has been a dentist for twenty-two years. She is the one patients ask for when they're anxious — she is famously impossible to hurry.

**Dr. Sam Okafor** joined us in 2018 and looks after most of our crown and denture work. He sketches what he's proposing on paper before he touches anything.

**Jenny Park** is our hygienist. Half her appointments are people who were told off by a hygienist once and never went back. She doesn't tell people off.

**Rosa Delgado** runs the front desk, the diary, and — truthfully — the practice. If you call, you get Rosa, and Rosa sorts it out.

## Keeping it honest

We're happy to give a second opinion on any treatment plan, including ours. If something we suggest can safely wait, we'll say so — plenty of our advice is "leave it alone and we'll check it again in a year."
