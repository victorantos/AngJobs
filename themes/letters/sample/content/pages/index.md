I write one letter at a time and send it when it's finished, which is usually
every few weeks and occasionally sooner. Each one is an essay — short enough to
read with a cup of coffee, long enough to have been worth writing down.

If you're new, the [archive](/blog/) is arranged like a book's contents; start
anywhere, though [Why Letters](/blog/why-letters/) explains what this is and
[Three Miles an Hour](/blog/three-miles-an-hour/) is the one people forward.

The best way to read is by [email](/subscribe/): new letters arrive the
morning they're finished, and nothing else ever does. There's an
[RSS feed](/blog/rss.xml) too, for the beautifully old-fashioned.
