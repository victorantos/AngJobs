## News tips

Seen something? Ring **+1 555 0123** or write to **newsdesk@example.com**.
A journalist reads everything, usually the same day. If it needs to be
confidential, say so in the first line and we'll take it from there.

## Letters

Letters for publication should include your name and street (street only is
what we print). We edit for length, never for opinion. The best letters
respond to something we got wrong — see below.

## Corrections

We publish corrections at the same prominence as the error. If we got a
fact wrong, tell us plainly: **corrections@example.com**.

## Advertising

Local advertisers keep this paper free to read. Rates and deadlines:
**ads@example.com**. Advertising buys space in the paper — it does not buy
coverage in it, and the person answering this address can't change that.
