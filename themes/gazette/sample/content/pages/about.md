This paper is written by three journalists and a rotating cast of
correspondents who are paid in bylines and coffee. It is funded by readers
and by local advertising taken under one rule: advertisers buy space, never
coverage.

## The rules we report under

- **Names and places are real.** If a source needs anonymity, we say so and
  say why.
- **Corrections run where the error ran** — front page mistakes get front
  page corrections, at the same size.
- **We don't publish what we can't stand behind.** A story a day late and
  right beats one on time and wrong.
