Your status page now takes subscriptions. A visitor leaves an email,
confirms it, and gets incident updates and resolutions automatically — the
same text you post, no extra work during the incident, when extra work is
least affordable.

Privacy is the design: subscriber lists belong to the status page, not to
your marketing stack. You can count them; you can't export them. That line
is what makes "subscribe for updates" a fair ask during someone else's
worst hour.

On Team and Scale plans now, capped at 5,000 subscribers per page while we
watch deliverability.
