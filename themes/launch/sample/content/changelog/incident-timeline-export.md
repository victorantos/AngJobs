Postmortems die on timestamp archaeology: twenty browser tabs to establish
what fired when. Every incident now exports its complete timeline —
detection, alerts, acknowledgments, escalations, status posts, and any
notes — as Markdown, in chronological order, timezone-normalized.

It's deliberately a skeleton, not a generated report: the export gives you
the facts in order; the thinking is still your job. Paste it into your
postmortem template and spend the meeting on the *why*.
