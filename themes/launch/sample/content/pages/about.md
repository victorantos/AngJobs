Pulse is four people: two former SREs, one designer, and one person who
does everything else and won't be more specific. We carried pagers for
companies you've heard of and built the monitor we kept wishing existed —
one that pages like a good colleague: rarely, accurately, and with the
context already attached.

## How we run it

We're profitable, bootstrapped, and not for sale, which means the roadmap
is set by customers instead of a term sheet. The [changelog](/changelog/)
ships every week or two; when it goes quiet, assume something interesting
broke and read the next entry.

## The dogfood clause

Pulse monitors Pulse, from outside our own infrastructure, and our status
page runs on the same code yours does. The month we wouldn't trust our own
product to page us about itself is the month we stop charging for it.
