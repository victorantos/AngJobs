*Plans live in `data/pricing.json` — edit the file and this grid updates.
The notes below are for the questions the grid can't answer.*

**Every plan includes every region.** We don't sell geography; the free
plan checks from the same six regions as Scale.

**Annual pays for two months.** Any plan, minus 17%, cancel whenever —
prorated to the day, by design, because exit friction is a smell.

**Nonprofits and open-source projects:** the Team plan is free — email us
from the project's domain and it's done in one reply. No form, no renewal
theater.
