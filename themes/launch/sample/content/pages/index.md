## Checks that think like an engineer

Every 30 seconds, from six regions, with assertions — not just "is it 200"
but "does the response contain what production should say." Latency
percentiles per region, TLS expiry warnings two weeks out, and a checker
fleet we publish our own uptime for.

## Alerts that respect the on-call

One page per incident, not one per region. Escalation that follows your
rotation, quiet hours that hold until something is actually customer-facing,
and every alert carries the diff of what changed — so the 3am decision is
"ack or escalate," never "investigate from scratch."

## A status page customers believe

Incidents post automatically with real timestamps, and the history can't be
quietly edited afterward — which is exactly why customers trust it. Custom
domain, your logo, zero JavaScript required to read it.
