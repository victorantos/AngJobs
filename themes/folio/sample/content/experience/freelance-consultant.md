A year of short engagements between jobs, mostly performance work: the kind
where you arrive, measure, delete JavaScript, and leave a dashboard behind
so the numbers stay honest after you're gone.

The furniture-retailer checkout was the flagship: 4.2s to 2.0s on mid-range
phones, done with cuts rather than cleverness. The engagement report became
a template I still use — every recommendation paired with the metric it
moves and the date to check it.
