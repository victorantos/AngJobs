Make pragmatic tradeoffs between shipping speed and long-term system health.

Work in a high-trust, AI-assisted environment where you drive the vision.

7+ years of experience building&#x2F;maintaining high-traffic, full-stack systems.

Expertise in Elixir or an equivalent functional&#x2F;concurrent stack.

Strong foundation in observability, cloud infrastructure (AWS), and incident response.

Clear communication—you can turn complex technical ideas into diagrams and strategy.

Compensation: $120k–$190k base + meaningful equity.
