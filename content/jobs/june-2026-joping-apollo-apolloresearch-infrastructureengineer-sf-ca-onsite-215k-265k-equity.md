Apollo Research - Infrastructure Engineer - SF, CA - Onsite - $215k–$265k + equity

You’d build the infrastructure (IaC, networking, observability) Apollo depends on, decide the controls that keep pre-release models safe and have a direct impact on our relationships with frontier labs.
