Kinelo - kinelo.com - San Francisco - Onsite (with a bit of flexibility)

Kinelo solves &quot;context myopia&quot;: autonomous and semi-autonomous AI agents don&#x27;t know what they don&#x27;t know, so they can&#x27;t search or find the background information required to accomplish tasks accurately and effectively. The result looks like slop but it&#x27;s not due to model performance, more due to workflow integration. From there, we&#x27;re rapidly moving into organizing humans together with autonomous &quot;AI coworkers&quot; into workflows that span both. Our goal is to change how organizations are run and structured.

We&#x27;re hiring for two roles right now:

- Product Engineer &#x2F; Senior Software Engineer (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;job-boards.greenhouse.io&#x2F;kinelo&#x2F;jobs&#x2F;4205846009" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;job-boards.greenhouse.io&#x2F;kinelo&#x2F;jobs&#x2F;4205846009</a>) -- do you have the software engineering wisdom not yet encoded in an LLM? and do you have product sense and the willingness and desire to think several steps ahead in the market?

- Founding GTM Lead (<a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;job-boards.greenhouse.io&#x2F;kinelo&#x2F;jobs&#x2F;4215784009" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;job-boards.greenhouse.io&#x2F;kinelo&#x2F;jobs&#x2F;4215784009</a>) -- there&#x27;s no playbook for go-to-market in the category we&#x27;re creating + the landscape is changing daily. Come architect a strategy and then move mountains to execute on it and help us grow insanely fast.

Competitive salary, meaningful (~first batch of hires) equity, founder previously exited to Apple, strong fundraising history (~$10m).
