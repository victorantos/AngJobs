Assembly - Founding Engineer - New York City, Onsite - Full-Time

Assembly is a vertically-integrated, technology-native holding company that acquires industrial real estate. We take an AI-native approach across the full surface area of our business; data-based geography selection, owner outreach, due diligence, deal structuring &#x2F; scenario analysis, property &amp; tenant management, and more. Our intention is to build one of the largest real estate companies in the world, and to be the most technology-native real estate owner in history.

We are hiring a Founding Engineer (Full-Stack) to design and build our proprietary technology stack from inception. You will own the development of our tech stack and have significant autonomy to build. Experience with Python, React &#x2F; TS, LLM APIs, data pipelines, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, web scraping and entity resolution are relevant.

Cash compensation of $250K - $350K with meaningful equity compensation as the Founding Engineer of a new business.

To apply, please reach out to swc[at]assemblygh.com.
