Zenskar - Bengaluru, India (Hybrid – 2 days&#x2F;week in-office) - Senior Backend Engineer

Zenskar is an AI-native revenue automation platform built to handle real-world complexity.

Looking for engineers with 5–7 years of experience building production backend systems, strong system design instincts, and comfort operating in high-scale, high-complexity environments.

Tech: backend systems, distributed architecture, APIs, AWS, event-driven systems
