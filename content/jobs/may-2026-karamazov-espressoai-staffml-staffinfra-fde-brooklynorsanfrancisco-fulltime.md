Espresso AI - Staff ML, Staff Infra, FDE - Brooklyn or San Francisco - Full time

We&#x27;re using LLMs to build neural optimizers, neural scheduling systems, and neural workload tuners. (If you&#x27;re ex-Google, you can think of it like Borg powered by LLMs.)

Today we use ML to make data warehouses and spark jobs more efficient. We&#x27;re hiring staff ML engineers to train models that can understand how much compute a job needs, how it scales to larger machines, whether a machine can run more jobs, and so on; and staff infra engineers to take those models and deploy them on real-world production systems.

We&#x27;re also looking for FDEs who can help us talk to users and run pilots. This is a pretty technical role (you need to be able to do data analysis and debug in prod) that&#x27;s also user-facing - it should be a good fit for a former (or future) technical founder.
