Saildrone - Multiple Roles - Onsite&#x2F;Hybrid (US Only) - SF Bay Area (Alameda) - Full-Time - <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.saildrone.com&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.saildrone.com&#x2F;</a>

Saildrone is the world&#x27;s leading operator of uncrewed surface vehicles (USVs), providing persistent in situ data from the most challenging ocean environments on Earth. Our fleet, ranging from the 23-foot Explorer to the brand-new 170-foot Spectre, is revolutionizing how we understand and secure our oceans. We work with global navies to deliver maritime domain awareness, partner with governments to map the seafloor in high-resolution, and collaborate with scientists to track climate change and hurricane intensification in real-time.

Maybe you saw that we recently unveiled Spectre, a new class of high-speed USV designed for strike and anti-submarine warfare[1]. Or perhaps you read about our partnership with Meta, where we completed the first-of-its-kind autonomous deep-water cable survey[2]. You might have also seen our ongoing mission with NOAA to map 13,000 square miles of the Mariana Islands[3].

Whether we are hucking drones into Category 4 hurricanes or scaling up the production of the world’s largest autonomous wingsails, we are looking for people who want to build the future of maritime autonomy.

We&#x27;re hiring for a breadth of different positions across our organization. In software, we&#x27;re looking for folks in our Perception, Platform&#x2F;Frontend, and Vehicle teams. Stacks vary, but we&#x27;re looking primarily for backgrounds in C++ and&#x2F;or JavaScript (Node, TypeScript, React). All software positions are hybrid roles based out of our HQ in Alameda, just a quick ferry ride across the Bay from San Francisco.

In addition to software, we are also actively hiring for roles in:

* Production &amp; Manufacturing (Composite technicians, assembly, and electronics)

* Fleet Operations (Mission managers, vehicle pilots, etc.)

* Hardware Engineering (Mechanical, electrical, and systems)

You can see a list of open positions on our career page: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.saildrone.com&#x2F;careers" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.saildrone.com&#x2F;careers</a>

Please apply through our career page; be sure to select &quot;Other&quot; and specify this HN post when asked how you heard about us!
