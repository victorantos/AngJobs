CHEERS.FAN #1 - Founding Infrastructure Engineers - Bay Area &#x2F; Remote

CHEERS.FAN #1 is an early-stage company exploring infrastructure challenges involving real-time coordination, distributed systems, and large-scale interactive experiences.

We are seeking founding engineers in three broad areas:

1. Distributed Systems &amp; Platform Engineering

Building reliable, scalable systems involving distributed coordination, event-driven architectures, cloud infrastructure, networking, and high-concurrency platforms.

2. Real-Time Synchronization &amp; Timing Systems

Building systems where timing, synchronization, low-latency communication, deterministic behavior, and real-time coordination matter.

3. Embedded &amp; Edge Infrastructure Engineering

Building infrastructure that operates close to devices, networks, and the edge, including embedded systems, firmware, networking, and performance-sensitive environments.

Experience with technologies such as Go, Rust, C++, Kafka, Kubernetes, WebRTC, networking, streaming infrastructure, distributed coordination systems, embedded systems, or related technologies is valuable.

We are particularly interested in engineers who have built large-scale, reliable systems where timing, coordination, performance, and resilience matter.

If that sounds interesting, we&#x27;d welcome a conversation.
