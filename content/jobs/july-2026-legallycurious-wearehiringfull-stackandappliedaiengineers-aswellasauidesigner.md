We are hiring full-stack and applied AI engineers, as well as a UI designer, for in-person roles in NYC.

Our product helps everyone make better legal decisions by better understanding the law. About one-third of important U.S. law is not accessible via ChatGPT or Claude. Much AI-generated legal analysis is wrong. We are building one of the largest collections of U.S. law in existence and using AI to make it make sense.

We are a seed-stage start-up, still in stealth (but already with an acquisition offer!), backed by tier-1 VCs and many of the most prominent experts in legal technology. We are working with several of the best law firms in the world. We have strong customer demand and need people to help us fill it. We are committed to improving access to justice.

We are hiring a small team of extremely talented, motivated, intense, and interesting people to solve the most difficult and important problem in legal tech: high quality answers to legal questions. Expect market-leading compensation, including meaningful equity ownership. Please send your resume to contact[at]ashmere.com. We are ONSITE only in NYC.
