Colonist - Senior Product Engineer, QA Engineer - Full-time - Remote &amp; async

Colonist (colonist.io) is a multiplayer board game platform. ~20 people, millions of monthly players, no middle management between you and the product.

You&#x27;ll own features end-to-end and ship to a live player base within days. Right now we&#x27;re evolving the core experience and building toward a multi-game platform. The mission is to become the internet&#x27;s biggest board game platform.

This role is a good fit if you: want to see your work matter at real scale without a large team around you, are comfortable with ambiguity and don&#x27;t need someone to define your tasks, and use AI tools as a genuine multiplier in your workflow.

This isn&#x27;t a good fit if you: prefer a larger team with specialists for every layer, or are looking for a role with clearly scoped tickets.
