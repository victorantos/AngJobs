Relevant Healthcare - Software Engineers (multiple levels) - Remote (US&#x2F;Canada) - Full-time

Relevant builds a data platform that helps safety-net healthcare providers deliver better care using data from their electronic health records. Our customers are non-profit clinics called Community Health Centers. You can read more about them, us, and the roles on our jobs page: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;relevant.healthcare&#x2F;jobs&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;relevant.healthcare&#x2F;jobs&#x2F;</a>

We&#x27;re a small team (~25 overall). We&#x27;re bootstrapped (no external capital whatsoever, VC or otherwise) and profitable. We&#x27;ve got a modern tech stack (Rails, Typescript, React, GraphQL, d3) and a healthy approach to both tech debt and work&#x2F;life balance… and we’re also a very nice group of people that get to work on interesting problems for some of the few unimpeachably good actors in healthcare!
