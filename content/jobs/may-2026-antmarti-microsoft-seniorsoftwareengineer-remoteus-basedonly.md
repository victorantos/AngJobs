Microsoft - Azure Deployments &amp; Bicep Team - Senior Software Engineer - REMOTE (US-based only)

The Azure Deployments &amp; Bicep team aims to make deploying resources to Azure simple, safe, predictable, and reliable. We own the backend services powering Azure deployments, Bicep DSL, and various other services aimed at helping customers be successful with Infrastructure as Code. We are at the heart of the Azure Resource Manager control plane with great opportunities for cross-team collaboration and impact, have a lot of autonomy to shape the direction of our own products, and enjoy having a hands-on approach with customers to help drive our feature design.

We&#x27;re looking for a Senior Software Engineer to help maintain and build automation around our Azure Verified Modules offering in the short-term, but with the long-term goal of transitioning to work across all our core products. You will act as a lead for new features and functionality, as well as providing mentorship to junior colleagues. We&#x27;re not looking for any specific language experience, but are looking for demonstrable leadership, mentoring and collaboration skills.

This role is only open to candidates with US work authorization.
