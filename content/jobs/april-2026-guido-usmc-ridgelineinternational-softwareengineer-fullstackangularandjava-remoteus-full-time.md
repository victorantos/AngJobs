Ridgeline International - Software Engineer, Full Stack (Angular and Java) - REMOTE (US) - Full-Time

Ridgeline International builds software that fights Ubiquitous Technical Surveillance (UTS), which is the growing threat of everyday commercial devices, apps, and networks being used to passively collect data on people and organizations without their knowledge. Think smartphones, smart TVs, IoT devices, and third-party apps leaking sensitive behavioral and location data at scale. Our tools help U.S. government and enterprise customers understand and manage that exposure, often before they even realize they have a problem.

This is a genuinely full-stack role. You will own features from design through deployment, working with Java (backend), Angular (frontend), REST and GraphQL APIs, SQL databases, and cloud infrastructure with DevOps workflows. For the first four months you will support legacy systems built on C# and Azure, then transition to our modern core platform. That bridge phase is intentional, not a bait-and-switch.

What you need:

*U.S. citizenship required, ability to obtain a security clearance
*5 or more years of professional software development
*Strong OOP fundamentals and relational database experience
*Comfort with REST APIs and a genuine growth mindset

Bonus if you have: Java or Angular experience, GIS or mapping work, DevOps and CI&#x2F;CD, or containerization (Docker, Kubernetes).

Perks include flexible PTO, a generous 401k match up to 10% with automatic 3% safe harbor, full health benefits, HSA employer contribution, professional coaching, and personal privacy protection services.

We are the hiring company, not a recruiter.
