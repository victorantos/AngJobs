Treasure AI - Staff Engineers (Fullstack&#x2F;Backend) - HYBRID (Tokyo &amp; Vancouver) - Full Time - <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.treasure.ai&#x2F;" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;www.treasure.ai&#x2F;</a>

We need three Staff Engineers (two in Tokyo, one in Vancouver) to act as technical anchors. You’ll be a great fit if you’re a backend&#x2F;distributed systems engineer who can architect a clean React frontend, or a pure distributed systems specialist happy to collaborate across the full stack.

The roles primarily deal with architecting systems to process and personalize massive marketing datasets in near real time, with a strong emphasis on being able to work cross functionally to deliver best-in-class agentic interactions deeply integrated with our systems.

Our core stack is Rails, Typescript&#x2F;Lambda, React, and Kotlin.
