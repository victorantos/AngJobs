We are a DIY distribution and promotion platform for independent artists and labels. In an industry that&#x27;s notoriously over-complicated and dominated by players who only care about volume, we&#x27;re building something kinder: a genuinely helpful way to release music and build a following.

We already support more than 20,000 users in their music careers. In the Netherlands, we&#x27;re a leading provider of independent digital music distribution, and we continue to grow steadily on an international scale.

We are actively looking for a senior full stack engineer and a medior operations and automation engineer. Unfortunately we can’t offer relocation or visa sponsorship.

Our stack relies on python, fastapi, postgres, next.js, react, typescript, gcp, terraform, k8s, redis, dataflow, see job descriptions for more details.
