Revio Insight - Data Engineer - Remote (US) - Full Time

We are seeking a Data Engineer to design, build, and maintain the data infrastructure that powers our platform, which serves community banks. This role will focus on building reliable and scalable pipelines to ingest, process, and transform core bank data. They will also design integrations of our data with CRMs and marketing automation platforms.

Our platform primarily receives structured data from customers in CSV format delivered via SFTP, which must be validated, transformed, and loaded into our AWS-based data infrastructure. The Data Engineer will own the reliability, performance, and scalability of these pipelines while ensuring strong data quality and security practices appropriate for financial data.

This role will work closely with product, engineering, implementation, and customer teams to ensure accurate and timely data processing that supports both customer-facing functionality and internal analytics.

To apply, email dataengineer [at] getrevio [dot] ai.
