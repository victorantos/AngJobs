MWI Animal Health - Remote (US only) - multiple roles

MWI Animal Health is a leading U.S. distributor of animal health products, medications, and technology solutions for veterinarians and livestock producers.

1. Senior Application Developer (React + Composable Commerce)

We&#x27;re hiring a senior dev (8+ yrs) to lead projects, mentor juniors, and build scalable apps end-to-end. Stack: React (Redux&#x2F;Context), C#&#x2F;.NET, microservices, headless commerce (CommerceTools, ContentStack), NoSQL + relational DBs, event streaming, TDD. You&#x27;ll turn messy business requirements into solid technical solutions and help shape our architecture. Bonus: WMS experience. Apply if you like leading, mentoring, and shipping.

2. QA Engineer

We&#x27;re hiring a QA Engineer to own quality across our suite of connected web applications and the shared services between them. You&#x27;ll mix hands-on manual&#x2F;exploratory testing with building and maintaining automated suites (end-to-end, integration, component), with a big focus on the seams between apps: navigation, shared auth, data hand-offs, and micro-frontend integrations. You&#x27;ll own the full bug lifecycle, wire tests into CI&#x2F;CD as reliable quality gates, and partner with product and engineering to build testability in from the start.

Ideal fit: 3+ years in QA testing web apps, across both manual and automated testing with modern frameworks (Playwright, Selenium) and unit&#x2F;component tooling (Jest, Vitest). Comfortable testing SPAs and REST APIs, with enough JavaScript&#x2F;TypeScript to write and debug test code. Bonus points for React, Vite, monorepos, micro-frontends, GitHub Actions. Above all, a user-focused mindset — catching problems before customers do.

3. Senior DevOps Engineer

We&#x27;re hiring a Senior DevOps Engineer to standardize application deployments, containerization, and release processes across our org. You&#x27;ll build shared CI&#x2F;CD pipelines, containerize apps with Docker&#x2F;Kubernetes, and create the tooling and golden paths that let teams ship reliably. Ideal fit: solid experience with CI&#x2F;CD (GitHub Actions&#x2F;GitLab CI&#x2F;Jenkins), IaC (Terraform&#x2F;Helm), and cloud providers (AWS&#x2F;Azure), plus a track record of driving standardization at scale. Pragmatic, collaborative, and motivated by making other teams more productive.
