MeanderX, formerly Orbio Earth, YC S23 - Senior Full-Stack Engineer - Germany, London, or Cape Town preferred - Full-time

MeanderX is building AI-powered grid intelligence for renewable energy developers.

Bonus points for experience in power engineering, renewables, grid infrastructure, geospatial data, scraping, document AI, or large-scale data pipelines.

High ownership, very little bureaucracy, and real impact on renewable energy deployment.
