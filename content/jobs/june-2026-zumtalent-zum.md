Zūm - Senior&#x2F;Staff Software Engineer - Hybrid&#x2F;onsite (Redwood City, CA), Full-time

Zūm (ridezum.com) is reinventing K-12 student transportation in the U.S. — a $30B+ industry that moves 26 million kids every school day. Our Connected Mobility Experience (CMX) platform combines modern routing, real-time tracking, and an EV fleet to give school districts a smarter, more transparent alternative to legacy transportation providers. Backed by TPG, Sequoia, and SoftBank with $430M raised at a $1.7B valuation, Zum operates in major districts including San Francisco and Oakland Unified, where they deployed the first all-electric school bus fleet in the country.

We&#x27;re hiring Senior and Staff-level product-oriented backend software engineers as well as a Senior Product Manager.  These are hybrid&#x2F;onsite roles with an expectation of working from our amazing Redwood City HQ 3x&#x2F;week.
