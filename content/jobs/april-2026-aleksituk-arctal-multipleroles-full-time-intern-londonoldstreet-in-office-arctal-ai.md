Arctal - Multiple roles - Full-time + Intern - London (Old Street, in-office) - arctal.ai

AI agents are our product and our operations. We build structured datasets from financial documents using agent pipelines — and we run the company the same way. Our clients include some of the largest financial institutions in the world.

We wrote a manifesto about what it means to build a company when agents do the work: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;arctal.ai&#x2F;manifesto" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;arctal.ai&#x2F;manifesto</a>

Hiring:

- Founding Data Engineer: own ingestion, cleanup, storage, transformation, distribution. 3–5 yrs exp.

- AI Delivery Lead: lead a team of AI agents to deliver data and analysis at scale. 1–5 yrs exp.

- Founding Operator: own the GTM operational engine: sales, marketing, internal tooling, everything in between. 1–5 yrs exp.

- AI Delivery Intern: work with the delivery lead to ship datasets using agent workflows. ~3-6 months.

Early equity + competitive salary (negotiable) for all roles. Details: <a href="https:&#x2F;&#x2F;arctal.ai&#x2F;hiring" rel="nofollow">https:&#x2F;&#x2F;arctal.ai&#x2F;hiring</a>

We&#x27;re a team of five and will hire selectively to grow revenue over headcount. If the manifesto resonates, send me an email: aleksi@arctal.ai.
