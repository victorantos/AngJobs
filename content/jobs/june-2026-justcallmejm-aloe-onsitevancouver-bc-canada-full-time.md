Aloe - Multiple Engineering &amp; Research roles - ONSITE Vancouver, BC, Canada - Full-time

Aloe is the epistemic intelligence lab building AI we can trust with humanity&#x27;s most important applications. We work on research and products that bring introspection to AI and critical thinking to machines. Our Agent Models are designed for decision-making in the real world – AI that understands what it does and doesn&#x27;t know, reasons about its limitations, and directs its own learning to self-improve.

Please read the job description and include in your outreach why you fit not just the role but also our culture. We strive to be Level 5 humans, co-creating a deliberately developmental atmosphere.
