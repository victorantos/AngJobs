Kroo - Product and Integrations SWEs - NYC, Hybrid - Full-Time - $110k - $200k + equity + health benefits

Kroo is building the knowledge platform for construction, an industry that deeply needs more technology solutions. We’ve built a platform that our 30+ enterprise customers use to operate and maintain data connectors, warehousing, and knowledge graphs specific to the nuances of the construction industry (think on-premises systems, archaic APIs, etc.). Our customers, data professionals in the construction industry, use our software to build reporting and run analytics for billions of dollars of jobs.

We&#x27;re looking for full-stack Product Engineers with 3+ YOE and Integrations Engineers with 1+ YOE. We are a venture-backed, pre-seed stage company with an experienced and hungry team. We’re backed by leading investors in the space and based in Midtown NYC (hybrid). If you’re looking to build in an industry that desperately needs data and AI solutions, apply here!
