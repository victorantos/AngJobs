## Design Elements

The AngJobs brand features:
- **"AJ" monogram** with integrated upward arrow (symbolizing career growth)
- **Hacker News orange** as primary brand color (connecting to the source)
- **Clean, modern typography** (Segoe UI, professional yet approachable)
- **Tech-forward aesthetic** suitable for developer community
